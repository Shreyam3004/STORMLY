Stormly Enterprise© was established with the then name Thunder Stealth. It was founded in 2007 by Shreyam Pandey , then a Pennsylvania State University graduate student working on a master's degree in meteorology and Vandit Agarwal a graduate from Massachussetts Institute of Technology ,also working on his master's degree in meteorology in Pennsylvania State University . While running their company, they also worked as a member of Penn State's meteorology faculty. The company adopted the name "Thunder Stealth Enterprise" in 2009 with it's first CEO as V Pranay . It has operations in 62 countries and 123 offices worldwide.

Stormly© is headquartered in Bangalore, Karnataka, with offices at 7 Trade Centers in Delhi in addition to Hyderabad, Mumbai ,Kolkata and Raipur City,Chattisgarh. Internationally, Stormly has offices in Montreal, Dublin, Tokyo, Seoul, Beijing and New Jersey. Renamed Stormly Enterprise in 2017, it now houses specialized severe weather forecasters.
